We heal in nature — a sanctuary for people & animals

MyPlanetKarma is a 6-acre healing sanctuary where rescued animals roam freely, Ayurvedic herbs are grown organically and visitors recover through nature-based therapies. Spend time with animals, learn regenerative farming, and find calm in the healing gardens.

Organic Herb Farm

Our farm grows herbs used in Ayurvedic practice following natural processes — no chemical pesticides or synthetic fertilisers. Visitors learn propagation, tending and how herbs are used for wellbeing.

Animal Shelter & Care

A freedom-first policy: rescued animals and birds receive food, medical care and space to roam. Interaction with animals is a core part of Nature Healing therapy and workshops.

How You Can Help

Your support helps us hire caretakers, secure the property, and provide medical care and food. We welcome volunteers, donations and long-term partners.

  • Hire animal caretakers and medical staff
  • Install fencing and improve security
  • Feed and treat rescued animals
  • Support sustainable farm infrastructure
